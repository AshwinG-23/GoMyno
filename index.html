About Us

At GoMyno, we believe in unleashing the potential of every coder. Founded with a vision to foster a thriving community of programming enthusiasts, GoMyno is the epicenter for competitive coding and problem-solving at IIT Goa. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting your journey, our club is your gateway to mastering the art of algorithms and data structures.

Our Mission

To nurture a collaborative environment where students can hone their skills, compete at national and international coding competitions, and innovate solutions for real-world challenges.
